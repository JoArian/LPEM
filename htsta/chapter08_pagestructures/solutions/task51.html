Task 51

Research with your neighbor what the following tags are used for.

  • header: the very top part of the page where you can find more pages, search bar, sign up and usually the logo of the site
  • nav: top of the page (but billow header) where you can navigate to other pages
  • main: white space around the article
  • footer: the bottom of the page where you can sometimes find additional links and information
  • section: the main part of the page
  • article: the main part of the page which groups sections together
  • aside: a vertical part of the page on the right (the space where theres usually an ad)
  • div:a group

The Styles and Semantics category of the following page will help you: https://www.w3schools.com/tags/ref_byfunc.asp
