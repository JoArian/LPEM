Which format(s) should be used in the following cases:

  1. a small animation
    2. GIF, its the only one that can be animated
  2. a partially transparent image
    3. PNG or GIF because theyre the only formats that support transparency
  3. a photo of the Grand-Duc Henry
    4. TIFF because its the highest quality
  4. A diagram
    5. BMP or PNG
  5. a landscape
    6. TIFF or JPEG
  6. A statistic
    7. TIFF, PNG because theyre high quality and PNG support transparency or GIF but it doesnt have as many colors
