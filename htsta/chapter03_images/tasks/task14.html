Task 14 - Thumbnails

In Task 13 we have inserted the images in unchanged size. In practice, however, smaller images, so-called Thumbnails, are used to save bandwidth during transmission. If you click on the smaller image, a hyperlink will take you to the larger image.

  1. Use the solution of task 13 by copying it to task14.html
  2. First reduce all images to a width of 300px. Add the extension _tn to the file name.
  3. Now include the resized images on the page.
  4. Use hyperlinks to click on the images to get to the original images.
