Chapter 4 - Lists

We distinguish between two types of lists: ordered unordered and unordered lists.

We always use ordered lists (<ol>) when the order of the individual elements (<li>) is important. If, on the other hand, the order is unimportant, we use unordered lists (<ul>).

It is important to pay attention to the correct nesting of the individual tags, regardless of whether it is an ordered or unordered list.

Examples can be found in the tasks of this chapter.
